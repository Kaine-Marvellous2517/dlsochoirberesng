About Us

DLSO Choir Benin-East Region Benin City, Edo State NG, is God's own choir. We sing with one motive, to win and reconsile men with God. We do not sing for fame neither do we play our musical instruments to show to our audience how skilled we are at doing it, but all we do is to the glory of God.
