Requirements

To join this choir you must be:

  • Born again
    • Must have repented from your sins
    • Accepted Jesus as your Lord and Saviour
    • Surrendered all to Him
  • Disciplined
    • Able to meet up with the days of practice
    • Willing to go through rigorous training to make you a good singer
    • Not absent in church services
    • Reading your bible daily
