Just like in any other organization there are rules and regulations governing the choir. In order to maintain discipline and orderliness, these rules have been set.

Random rules

  • No watching of movies that do not edify
  • Dress decent, gorgeous and virtious
  • Avoid any form of indicipline
  • Avoid any work of the flesh e.g malice, envy, pride
  • Avoid keeping any ungodly relationship with anyone

Singing rules

  • No practice no singing
  • Pray in the song before going on stage
  • Sing not for the apraisers, but unto God

Academic rules

  • Be studious
  • When preparing for an exam you are exempted, to have enough time to study
  • Don't use choir as an excuss for your faliure
